# AI Video Prompt Templates
# 
# These are starting points for your AI video generation.
# Edit and customize for your needs.
#
# Tips:
# - Be specific about camera movement (pan left, zoom in, orbit)
# - Describe lighting and atmosphere
# - Include style references (cinematic, anime, photorealistic)
# - Specify quality modifiers (masterpiece, best quality, 4K)

## Basic Scene Prompts

### Nature Scene
A serene mountain lake at dawn, mist rising from the water surface, 
sunlight filtering through pine trees, cinematic lighting, 
slow camera pan from left to right, 4K quality

### Urban Scene
A bustling city street at night, neon lights reflecting on wet pavement,
people walking with umbrellas, cinematic noir style,
slow dolly zoom, volumetric fog

### Abstract
Flowing liquid mercury forming geometric shapes, 
reflective surface catching rainbow light, 
smooth camera orbit, abstract art style

## Video-Specific Modifiers
- Camera movement: slow pan, dolly zoom, orbit, crane shot, static
- Style: cinematic, anime, photorealistic, oil painting, watercolor
- Quality: masterpiece, best quality, highly detailed, 4K, 8K
- Atmosphere: dramatic lighting, soft lighting, volumetric fog, lens flare

## Negative Prompts (what to avoid)
blurry, low quality, distorted, deformed, watermark, text, logo
